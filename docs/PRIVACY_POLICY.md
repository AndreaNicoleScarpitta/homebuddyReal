# Privacy Policy for Home Buddy

**Last updated: January 2026**

## Overview

Home Buddy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our home maintenance assistant application.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (via Replit authentication)
- Display name
- Profile information from your Replit account

### Home Profile Data
- Home address (street, city, state, zip code)
- Year built
- Square footage
- Home type

### Usage Data
- Home systems you track
- Maintenance tasks and their status
- Budget and expense information
- Chat conversations with our AI assistant
- Contact form submissions

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide personalized home maintenance recommendations
- Track and remind you of maintenance tasks
- Power the AI assistant with context about your home
- Manage your budget and expense tracking
- Respond to your inquiries via the contact form
- Improve our services

## Third-Party Services

### Replit Authentication
We use Replit for user authentication. Their privacy policy applies to authentication data: https://replit.com/site/privacy

### OpenAI
We use OpenAI's API to power our AI chat assistant. Chat messages are sent to OpenAI for processing. See their privacy policy: https://openai.com/privacy

### Resend (Optional)
If configured, we use Resend for email notifications. See their privacy policy: https://resend.com/privacy

### Google Places API (Optional)
If configured, we use Google Places for address autocomplete. See their privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### USPS Address Verification (Optional)
If configured, we use USPS APIs for address verification.

## Data Storage

- Your data is stored securely in our PostgreSQL database
- We use industry-standard security measures
- Data is hosted on Replit's infrastructure

## Data Retention

- We retain your data as long as your account is active
- You can request deletion of your data at any time
- Chat history is retained for conversation continuity

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data
- Opt out of non-essential data collection

## Children's Privacy

Home Buddy is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Contact page in the app.
